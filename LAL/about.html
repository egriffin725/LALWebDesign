About

This website provides an overview of the Surrealism art movement from 1925 to 1970, featuring a timeline of key Surrealist artists, notable artworks, and historical context. It serves as an interactive resource, linking to archival collections, museum holdings, and further reading materials to support research on the artists and topics within the movement.

Designed as an accompaniment to the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Surrealism entry in the Heilbrunn Timeline of Art History, this site aims to offer concise and informative overviews of Surrealist artists from various periods. While not an exhaustive list, the timeline connects users to deeper insights into the evolution of Surrealism, guiding both researchers and those curious about the movement's rich history.

LAL Web Design

Composed of Lindsey White, Alex Segur, and Liz Griffin, LAL Web Design is a collective of information professionals dedicated to helping researchers connect with art through interactive exhibits.
