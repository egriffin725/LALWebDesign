Museum Collections

Want to explore more?

Links to digital collections:

  • Museum of Modern art - MoMa

    The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) is renowned for its comprehensive collection of modern and contemporary art, showcasing works from a diverse range of movements and artists that have shaped the course of art history.

  • The Guggenheim Museum Online Collection

    The Guggenheim Museum's collection includes a significant selection of Surrealist art, featuring iconic works by artists such as Salvador Dalí, René Magritte, and Max Ernst, showcasing the movement's exploration of the unconscious, dream imagery, and the juxtaposition of unexpected elements.

  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Collection Database

    Covering 5,000 years of history, the Met offers over 490,000 works of art including detailed images, metadata, and historical context from diverse cultures and time periods.

  • The Getty Museum Collection

    A world-renowned repository of European and American art, spanning from ancient to contemporary periods, with a focus on European paintings, sculptures, decorative arts, and photography.



Links to digital libraries:

  • ARTSTOR

    A comprehensive digital library of high-quality images from museums, archives, and libraries worldwide. It provides access visual materials across various disciplines, including fine art, architecture, and decorative arts.
    Content: Over 2 million images of art, architecture, and cultural artifacts.

  • Eurpeana

    A digital platform that provides access to millions of digitized items from European cultural heritage institutions, including art museums, libraries, and galleries.
    Content: Digital collections of artworks, manuscripts, music, and more from cultural institutions across Europe.

  • The Getty Research Institute's Digital Resources

    Provides access to a wide array of digital resources for art history, including the Getty Vocabulary Program, digitized collections, and scholarly publications.
    Content: Resources on art history, visual culture, and the museum field, including archives and digitized materials.

  • Smithsonian Institution Collections Search Center

    A powerful database that allows users to search across the Smithsonian's vast collection of art, artifacts, and scientific objects.
    Content: Over 13 million records across the Smithsonian’s museums, libraries, and archives, including art and cultural collections.
