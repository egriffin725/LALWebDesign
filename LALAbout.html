LAL WEB DESIGN'S SURREALISM TIMELINE

ABOUT

This website is meant as an accompaniment to the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Surrealism entry in the Heilbrunn Timeline of Art History. As an organization, our goal is to provide succinct, helpful overviews of surrealist artists from different periods of the movement to guide research and curiousity.

While not an exhaustive list of artists, this timeline is meant to connect interested users with more information about what each era of the Surrealist movement looked like.

LAL Web Design

Composed of Lindsey White, Alex Segur, and Liz Griffin, LAL Web Design is a collective of information professionals dedicated to helping researchers connect with art through interactive exhibits.
